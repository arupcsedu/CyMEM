The Elephanta caves are " of unknown date and attribution " . Art historians have dated the caves in the range of late 5th to late 8th century AD . Archaeological excavations have unearthed a few Kshatrapa coins dated to 4th century AD . Elephanta was then called Puri or Purika , and served as the capital of the Konkan Mauryas .
