In 1941 , German policy evolved further , calling for the complete destruction of the Polish people , whom the Nazis regarded as " subhumans " ( Untermenschen ) . Within ten to twenty years , the Polish territories under German occupation were to be entirely cleared of ethnic Poles and settled by German colonists . The Germans hoped that a more lenient cultural policy would lessen unrest and weaken the Polish Resistance . Restrictions on education , theater and music performances were eased .
